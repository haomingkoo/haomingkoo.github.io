About Haoming Koo

Haoming Koo is an AI engineer and digital transformation leader based in Singapore. He currently works at AI Singapore as an Associate AI Engineer in the AI Apprenticeship Programme (AIAP). He has 7+ years of experience delivering AI-enabled programs across semiconductor manufacturing, cloud infrastructure, and data platforms.

At Micron Technology (2018-2025), Haoming led cross-site digital transformation across four fabrication facilities (Singapore, Boise, Hiroshima, Taichung), aligning 3,000+ engineers and driving USD 600M+ in business impact. He co-led the LPDDR5X yield ramp for Apple iPhone 16 Pro Max and the HBM3E yield program for NVIDIA H200/Blackwell GPUs.

Education: MSc in Smart Industries and Digital Transformation from the National University of Singapore (GPA 4.85/5.00). BSc (Honours) in Chemistry and Nanoscience from NUS (Distinction). Full Stack Development with AI from NUS x Emeritus. PMP certified. GA100 Generative AI from Heicoders Academy.

Awards: Micron Innovation Award for ResNet-50 computer vision model. Micron Idea of the Quarter for digital twin achieving $7M+ savings. Micron Culture Champion for cross-site collaboration.

Technical skills: Python, PyTorch, TensorFlow, scikit-learn, YOLOv8, OpenCV, MediaPipe, LightGBM, SHAP, CNN, LSTM, FastAPI, React, Flask, Docker, Google Cloud Platform, AWS, MLflow, Airflow, GenAI, RAG, LLM fine-tuning, agentic AI.

Portfolio projects: Trader Koo (S&P 500 AI market dashboard), LionWeather (Singapore weather ML forecasting), Preflight (automated EDA and ML baseline), Handwriting Lab (PyTorch digit recognition), Photo Compliance Studio (passport photo AI checks), MinMax Wine (three-way wine price comparison).
