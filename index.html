About

I build production AI systems that solve real operational problems. My work spans LLM-based assistants, agentic workflows, computer vision, and time-series modelling, deployed end-to-end with inference APIs, evaluation pipelines, and cloud infrastructure.

At Micron Technology (6+ years), I designed and deployed AI systems across four global fabs: a GenAI search assistant serving ~3,000 engineers, ML risk-scoring preventing $100M+ in losses, a ResNet-50 vision system improving yield by 4-5%, and a digital twin saving $7M+. These contributed to $600M+ in business impact.

In AI Singapore's AIAP, I build full-stack ML systems from scratch: CNNs, LSTMs, RAG pipelines deployed with CI/CD, containerised inference, and MLOps workflows. I also built Job Hunter SG: a 7-stage LLM resume tailoring platform with hallucination guardrails and tens of thousands of live job listings.

I'm drawn to the intersection of frontier AI and messy enterprise reality: scoping with users, shipping 0-to-1 prototypes, hardening them for production, and closing the loop with eval-driven feedback.
