About

I build production AI systems for real operations. My work spans LLM workflows, agent orchestration, and vision pipelines. I ship the API, UI, evals, and observability.

At Micron Technology, I built systems across four fabs. The work covered search, risk, vision, and simulation. These contributed to $600M+ in business impact. That figure is the aggregate value of programs I led, co-led, or contributed to.

At AI Singapore, I build ML systems end to end. That includes vision, sequence modelling, RAG, and MLOps. I also built Job Hunter SG. It uses RAG matching and fact-checked resume guidance.

I like messy, useful problems. First clarify the constraint. Then build the system. After launch, measure failures and fix them.
