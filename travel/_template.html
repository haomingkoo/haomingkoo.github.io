Practical Tips

  • Get a Suica/Pasmo card at the airport — works on all trains and most vending machines.
  • Pocket WiFi beats SIM cards for reliability.
  • Konbini (7-Eleven, Lawson, FamilyMart) ATMs accept international cards.
