# Claude Review Prompt

Use this prompt to get a second opinion on the portfolio and hiring page.

```text
You are a brutally honest portfolio reviewer for frontier AI companies, applied AI teams, startup founders, and technical clients.

Review my personal site:
- Home: https://kooexperience.com/
- Hire page: https://kooexperience.com/hire.html
- About: https://kooexperience.com/about.html
- GitHub: https://github.com/haomingkoo
- LinkedIn: https://linkedin.com/in/haomingkoo

Context:
I am Haoming Koo, an AI engineer in Singapore. I have 7+ years at Micron Technology, USD 600M+ business impact, AI Singapore AIAP experience, and several live products across LLM workflows, computer vision, weather ML, data products, maps, scraping, and automation. I want to be considered for serious AI engineering roles, applied AI/product engineering roles, forward deployed engineering roles, frontier AI companies such as Anthropic/OpenAI-style teams, and scoped client projects.

Please critique the site as if you were deciding whether to interview or hire me.

Focus on:
1. Does the first 10 seconds make me look credible, hireable, and differentiated?
2. Does the story feel human and specific, or does it sound like generic AI-generated portfolio copy?
3. Which claims feel strong, and which claims need more proof?
4. Does the new hire page make me look like someone who can build useful AI systems with societal impact?
5. What would make a recruiter, founder, or AI team hiring manager contact me?
6. What should be removed because it is distracting, boastful, vague, or weak?
7. What specific section, copy, interaction, or project-case-study improvements would most increase inbound LinkedIn/email outreach?

Return:
- A score from 1-10 for employability impact.
- A score from 1-10 for originality/character.
- The top 10 fixes in priority order.
- Rewritten hero copy if you think mine can be stronger.
- A short positioning statement I can reuse on LinkedIn.
```
